A little summary

Richard loves all things gaming, technology, and coding. He first started out learning c#.net in the early 2010's, and moved on from that into the networking space getting an A+ cert.

He then started doing private consulting for WordPress for integrations, plugins, and some custom HTML/CSS work. He is now pursuing and went all in on Fullstack development!

While Richard is pursuing his Fullstack Development Training and Career, he is also working on his dream. He has established a Indie Video Game Studio. They are focusing on their first game, which is an RPG (role-playing game).

With a current 2 person army, we are aiming for mid 2023 for our early access launch. If you want to follow our progress as we build this out, follow us here: Shattered Games Studios
